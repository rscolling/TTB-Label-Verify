One photo or hundreds — up to 300 at a time. JPG or PNG work best.

2 Add the submittal form (CSV)

A CSV file with these columns: filename, brand, class_type, abv, net_contents, producer, origin_country, is_import. We match each row to a photo by its file name.

No spreadsheet? We’ll still scan the photos and fill in what we read, but every row will be flagged for human review — there’s nothing to check the labels against.
